The Future of AI-Native Commerce

The way commerce works is about to change — radically.

AI agents are becoming the new buyers.
Consumers won't "browse" — they'll delegate.
Brands won't "optimize funnels" — they'll publish once and be discovered everywhere.
Commerce is no longer a platform. It's a protocol.
Commerce Mesh Protocol (CMP) is the open standard powering this future.

A New Infrastructure Layer for Commerce

CMP defines four core nodes that together form the fabric of decentralized, programmable commerce:

  • Discover: Publish once. Be found everywhere. Search is no longer gated.
  • Transact: AI-initiated, trustless commerce via open APIs.
  • Trust: Identity, and reputation — modular and programmable.
  • Fulfillment: Distributed routing and shipping logic that meets the buyer where they are.

Instead of walled-garden marketplaces, CMP enables a composable network of brands, agents, apps, and infrastructure.
